Welcome to Proxima

Proxima is a platform focusing on two main criteria - providing IT-related services to those who need assistance and on skill development where one can enroll in the courses that we offer to increase your knowledge and polish your skills.
If you are here on our page, that means you are either looking for some sort of assistance with your job/business or you are here because of your curiosity to learn.
We offer assistance with Photos and video editing, Logo designing, Business card, Banner design, App development and Software development and Proxima aims to reduce your cost and hassle by ensuring that you get most of your software or IT related work from one place instead of going or approaching different places for different tasks.
When it comes to our courses, we offer Introduction to Java, Introduction to Python, Introduction to Bootstrap, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator and Video Editing. Proxima tries to make learning as accessible as possible. Please check the “Courses” section for more details.
